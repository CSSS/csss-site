When you see "[By|General|SFSS] Elections", that means you have to substitute it with either "By-Elections" or "General Election" or "SFSS Council Rep Election"
  • It is an SFSS Council Rep Election if its the routine during the Spring term for a new SFSS Council Rep Election
  • It is a General Election if its the routine time during the Spring term for new elections
  • otherwise, it is a By-Election

4.1. Setting up Websurvey

4.1.1. General

  • Survey Name: 2021-2022 CSSS [By|General|SFSS] Elections
  • Completed Text: Thank you for casting your vote for our future Executive Officers!
  • Completed URL: https://sfucsss.org/

4.1.2. Questions


  • Heading: Nominee Speeches
  • Text : <a href="<election_url>" target="_blank"> Nominee Speeches</a>
      • election_url is the page for the current election on sfucsss.org, like https://sfucsss.org/elections/2021-04-06-general_election/

4.1.3. Access

  • Start Date: 2021-03-01 12:00 AM
  • End Date: 2021-03-20 12:00 AM
  • SFU Maillist: cmpt-ugrad, csss-honorary, cmpt-minors, cmpt-majors, cmpt-students

5.2. Save Results

5.2.1. Sharing results with CSSS

  1. Click on My Surveys
  2. Click on Edit for the relevant election
  3. Click on Administrators
  4. Enter csss for the computing ID of a new administrator and then Add Administrator
  5. Click on Save Changes

    6. 5.2.2. Sharing results with CSSS Website

    1. Click on View Submissions
    2. Click on Export for the correct websurvey
    3. Click on Click Here to Download Exported Records
    4. Go to https://sfucsss.org/elections/
    5. Click on Import Websurvey Results
    6. Then you can follow through the workflow
    7. You can verify that you did it correct by ensuring that the end results in the election is the same as what you see if you click on View under Summary for an election instead of Export in step 2
