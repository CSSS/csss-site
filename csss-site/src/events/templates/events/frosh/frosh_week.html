Frosh Week

'

FroshWeek is the ultimate introductory experience for Computing Science students at Simon Fraser University.

Organized by us, the CSSS, in partnership with the School of Computing Science, it is a week of fun-filled activities designed to orient, entertain and educate new university students with a Computing Science twist.

Held the week right before the start of the Fall Semester (August 31st - Sept 4th), FroshWeek is organized by students, for students. Incoming students receive: an in-depth introduction to the facilities available to them as Computing Science students; many entertaining on- and off-campus challenges and diversions to motivate, amuse, and connect them with other students; valuable advice from their elders with respect to courses and the university experience in general; and some tools to make the transition to university much smoother.
