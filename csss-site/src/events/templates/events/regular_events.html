Regular Events

You will be alerted to the next regular event, both via email and also on the announcements channel of the CSSS Discord Guild.

General Meetings

Every 3-4 weeks, the CSSS holds meetings to go over the following things:
  • Officer Reports: officer will report on anything that has happened, pertaining to their job that is necessary to announce at a meeting.
  • Announcements: Allow for users to be notified of any upcoming big events that may be happening.
  • Open Floor: Open Floor provides an opportunity for anyone at the meeting to voice a question, comment or concern about the CSSS, the CS Department, SFSS, etc.
there may also be other agenda items from time to time.

Board Games Nights

The BoardGames nights happen generally every 2-3 weeks on Friday. If you want to request a particular boardgames, please contact the Assistant Director of Events.

Movie Nights

The BoardGames nights happen generally every 2-3 weeks on Friday. If you want to request a particular movie, please contact the Assistant Director of Events.
