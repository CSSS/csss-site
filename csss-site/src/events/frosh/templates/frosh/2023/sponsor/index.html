About

What is FROSH?

FROSH is a series of events run by the Computing Science Student Society at Simon Fraser University in collaboration with the School of Computing Science.

FROSH week occurs during the fall semester to welcome new undergraduate students into the School of Computing Science. Starting your degree is a pivotal moment in each student's academic career, and with FROSH, the fondest memories are made.

RETRO FROSH is the next iteration of this tradition, starting September 11th and ending September 16th, 2023. This year's FROSH is inspired by SFU's School of Computing Science's 50th anniversary.

Events

The RETRO FROSH events take place over the course of five days and one night (midnight madness). These events have roots in the history of previously run FROSHes, but with an added twist to keep things fresh and exciting.

These events are:

  • Monday: Ice-Cream Social
  • Tuesday: Burnaby Mountain Park Hike
  • Wednesday: Info-Session, Pac Macro
  • Thursday: BBQ and Board Games
  • Friday: Midnight Madness

Ice-Cream Social

The ice-cream social is a recurring FROSH event where froshees socialize with fellow students over free ice-cream.

It is also during the ice-cream social that froshees receive their RETRO FROSH T-shirts, and meet their FROSH leaders.

Candy FROSH 2022, Ice-Cream Social

Burnaby Mountain Park Hike

This is an exciting adventure where froshees are invited to conquer Burnaby Mountain Park with a thrilling hike, games, and food.

This is a great opportunity for froshees to connect with their peers in an outdoor and active setting, creating lasting memories that will keep them excited for the rest of FROSH!

Candy FROSH 2022, Hike

Info-Session

During the info-session, froshees learn about Computing Science concentrations, SFU co-op, courses, and more from invited speakers.

This is an event intended to ease the transition from high-school to university by allowing froshees to ask questions and hear advice from experienced academics and professionals.

FROSH Week 2017, Info-Session

Pac-Macro

Pac-macro is a FROSH week classic. Froshees take turns playing as Pacman and Ghosts in the streets of downtown Vancouver.

CSSS-developed software is used to keep track of the location of players digitally, and present a Pacman-like map to players.

Candy FROSH 2022, Pac-Macro

BBQ and Board Games

During the welcome BBQ, we provide free burgers and hotdogs to froshees, grilled by our finest in-society chefs!

This well-earned meal is proceeded by a fun afternoon full of board games.

FROSH Week 2018, BBQ

Midnight Madness

Midnight Madness is the culmination of all events during FROSH week. Froshees are invited to stay overnight in the Applied Science Building for a full 12 hours of planned fun.

There will be challenges fought between the four Applied Science Schools (Computing Science, Engineering, Sustainable Engineering, Software Systems, and Mechatronics) Engineering, Software Systems, and Mechatronics), food, and much more.

One thing that has remained consistent with Midnight Madness since 2004 is the RAMMING CEREMONY, of which, by tradition, cannot be described. This is the ultimate initiation into SFU Computing Science.

(Disclaimer: no froshees have or will ever be harmed during the ramming ceremony; the name should not be taken upon its initial impression.)

Why Sponsor Us?

FROSH is an opportunity to influence Computer Scientists early on into their career. Sponsoring us enables you to:

  • Impact a large portion of new Computer Scientists early in their career.
  • Access the resumés of our engaged FROSH Leaders.
  • Display your company logo on our RETRO FROSH merchandise.
  • Impact the organization and theme of Pac Macro (GOLD or SILVER package).
  • Present during our Info-Session (GOLD package).

Funding FROSH also ensures that we can create memories for another year of passionate Computer Scientists.

Your contribution to FROSH will allow you to advertise your company in a variety of ways, depending on your Sponsorship Package.

👑 GOLD Package

Contribution of greater than $800, or equivalent value in merchandise or items.

The GOLD Package allows your company to make the greatest impact on our Froshees.

  • Present during our Info-Session.
  • Large-size logo on our RETRO FROSH merchandise.
  • Influence gameplay of Pac-Macro.
  • Choose names for characters in Pac-Macro
  • Access resumés of FROSH Leaders.
  • Inclusion as sponsor in RETRO FROSH media.

💍 SILVER Package

Contribution of greater than $400, or equivalent value in merchandise or items.

The SILVER Package allows your company to make a significant impact on our Froshees.

  • Medium-size logo on our RETRO FROSH merchandise.
  • Choose names for characters in Pac-Macro
  • Access resumés of FROSH Leaders.
  • Inclusion as sponsor in RETRO FROSH media.

PROVIDER Package

Contribution of less than $400, or equivalent value in merchandise or items.

The PROVIDER Package will allow your company to impact froshees and help make RETRO FROSH possible.

  • Inclusion as sponsor in RETRO FROSH media.

INDIVIDUAL Package

Contribution of $25.

The INDIVIDUAL Package is directed towards individuals that want to sponsor RETRO FROSH.

  • Leave your mark on RETRO FROSH merchandise with a small-scale mark or signature.
